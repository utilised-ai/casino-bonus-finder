Frequently Asked Questions

What is a casino welcome bonus?

A welcome bonus is a promotional offer that casinos give to new players when they sign up. It typically includes a match on your first deposit (e.g., 100% up to $1,000) and sometimes includes free spins or other perks.

What is a no deposit bonus?

A no deposit bonus gives you free casino credit or free spins just for registering, without requiring you to deposit any money. It's a risk-free way to try out a casino.

What are wagering requirements?

Wagering requirements (also called playthrough requirements) specify how many times you need to bet the bonus amount before you can withdraw any winnings. For example, a $10 bonus with 30x wagering would require $300 in bets before withdrawing.

Can I win real money from casino bonuses?

Yes, you can win real money from casino bonuses, but you'll need to meet the wagering requirements first. Always check the terms and conditions to understand what you need to do to withdraw your winnings.

How do I claim a casino bonus?

To claim a casino bonus, you typically need to register an account, verify your identity, and either enter a bonus code or make a qualifying deposit. Some no-deposit bonuses are credited automatically after registration.

Are casino bonuses worth it?

Casino bonuses can be worth it if you understand the terms and choose the right ones. Look for bonuses with reasonable wagering requirements and generous time limits to maximize their value.
